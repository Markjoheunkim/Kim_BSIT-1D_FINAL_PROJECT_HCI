Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

  • This degree program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills in understanding of computer systems, software development, and information technology.


Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

  • This degree combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical skills. The students will learn how to design and implement complex IT solutions, analyze data to make informed decisions, and troubleshoot technical issues.


Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing

  • This program focuses on the intersection of technology, creativity, and entertainment. It is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of multimedia production, interactive design, and digital media technologies.


Associate in Computer Technology

  • This program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems and technologies. The learners will learn about the latest trends in technology and how they can be applied in real-world situations.
