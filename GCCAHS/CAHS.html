VISION

By 2025, the College of Allied Health Studies is envisioned to be a center of development in Allied Health Education in Region 3 and dedicated to being a forerunner in providing research-inclined instruction, evidence- paseq- practice and community-responsive health care education committed to the holistic development of the human person.

MISSION


The College of Allied Health Studies aims to produce competent, highly skilled, and values- providers imbued with excellent professional and ethical standards who are able to meet the changing needs of the local, national, and global communities.

GOALS


The College of Allied Health Studies programs aims to produce a fully functioning and proficient health care provider who is abie to perform the competency: standards-based from the program outcomes in accordance to the existing issuance and guidelines of the Commission on Higher Education.
