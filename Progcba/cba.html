Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

  • This program focuses on developing students' skills in accounting, auditing, and financial reporting. It prepares graduates for the Certified Public Accountant (CPA) examination and careers in public accounting, corporate accounting, and government accounting. The curriculum includes courses in financial accounting, managerial accounting, taxation, and business law.


Bachelor of Science in Customs Administration

  • This program trains students in customs laws, tariff policies, and international trade regulations. It aims to produce graduates who are competent in customs brokerage, tariff management, and logistics. Students learn about import/export processes, customs procedures, and trade facilitation.


Bachelor of Science in Business Administration - Major in Financial Management

  • This course provides students with knowledge and skills in financial planning, investment analysis, and financial decision-making. Graduates are prepared for careers in banking, corporate finance, and financial consultancy. Key topics include financial markets, risk management, and financial statement analysis.


Bachelor of Science in Business Administration - Major in Human Resource Management

  • This program focuses on the strategic management of human resources within organizations. It covers areas such as recruitment, employee relations, performance management, and organizational development. Graduates can pursue careers in HR departments, recruitment agencies, and organizational consultancy.


Bachelor of Science in Business Administration - Major in Marketing Management

  • This course emphasizes marketing principles, consumer behavior, and strategic marketing planning. Students learn how to create and implement effective marketing strategies and campaigns. Career opportunities include roles in brand management, advertising, sales, and market research.
