Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

  • This program prepares students for careers in the hospitality industry, focusing on areas such as hotel and restaurant management, event planning, and customer service. The curriculum includes courses in hospitality operations, food and beverage management, and hospitality marketing. Graduates can pursue roles in hotels, resorts, restaurants, and other hospitality-related businesses.


Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management

  • This program focuses on the tourism industry, equipping students with knowledge in tourism planning, development, and management. The curriculum covers topics such as travel and tour operations, tourism marketing, and sustainable tourism. Graduates can work in travel agencies, tourism boards, resorts, and other sectors of the tourism industry.
