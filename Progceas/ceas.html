Bachelor of Elementary Education

  • It is a program that will learn about child development, classroom management techniques, educational psychology, and curriculum design. They will also gain practical experience through student teaching placements in elementary schools under the supervision of experienced educators.


Bachelor of Early Childhood Education

  • It is a program that will engage in coursework that covers topics such as early childhood curriculum design, assessment techniques, and classroom management strategies. They will also participate in field experiences and student teaching opportunities to gain practical experience working directly with young children.


Bachelor of Secondary Education - Major in English

  • It is a program that will study a wide range of subjects related to the English language, including grammar, composition, literature, and linguistics. They will also learn about teaching methodologies, classroom management techniques, and educational psychology


Bachelor of Secondary Education - Major in Filipino

  • It is a program will undergo a comprehensive curriculum that covers various aspects of Filipino language, literature, and culture. They will study topics such as grammar, syntax, semantics, phonology, morphology, and discourse analysis. In addition, they will also delve into the rich literary traditions of the Philippines through the study of different genres such as poetry, fiction, drama, and non-fiction.


Bachelor of Secondary Education - Major in Math

  • This degree will study a variety of mathematical topics including algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics, and more. They will also learn about pedagogy, curriculum development, assessment techniques, and classroom management strategies specific to teaching math in a secondary school setting.


Bachelor of Secondary Education - Major in Science

  • This program will take courses that cover various branches of science such as biology, chemistry, physics, and earth science. They will also study pedagogical theories and strategies that are specific to teaching science in a secondary school setting.


Bachelor of Secondary Education - Major in Social Studies

  • This program will take courses that cover a wide range of topics related to social studies education, including curriculum development, instructional strategies, assessment methods, and classroom management techniques.


Bachelor of Physical Education

  • It is a program that are exposed to a wide range of theoretical knowledge and practical skills that are essential for promoting physical activity and healthy living. They learn how to design effective exercise programs, assess individual fitness levels, analyze movement patterns, and develop strategies for improving athletic performance.


Bachelor of Cultural and Arts Education

  • This program will study a wide range of subjects, including art history, music theory, theater production, and cultural studies. They will also have the opportunity to participate in hands-on learning experiences such as internships and fieldwork placements.


Bachelor of Arts in Communication

  • This program is about literature, history, philosophy, visual arts, performing arts, media studies, journalism, public relations, and advertising. These courses are designed to help students develop their analytical skills, creativity, and ability to communicate effectively in various contexts.


Teacher Certificate Program

  • Obtaining a teacher certificate is essential for anyone who wishes to pursue a career in teaching. This certification ensures that teachers have met certain standards of competency and professionalism
